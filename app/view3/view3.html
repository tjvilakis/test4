About

TurboTwo.net came about as a university computer science project, but was a long time in the making. Several years ago, the site TurboSentry.net went down after the long time Sysop of the EntConn BBS's passed away [it is back up now thanks to Turbo Foot]. At the time I was still on active duty in the military and did not really have the time or knowledge to re-create or work on any MajorMud related projects. Fast forward to 2016, and I am finishing my Masters of CS at National University in San Diego and decided to write a new Telnet Crawler, to give myself a little more exposure to some technologies that I wasn't all that familiar with.

TurboTwo.net is written in Python for the Telnet crawling service which stores the Top10 list data from each board in JSON format on a MongoDB. The website you are looking at is written in Angular JS using Bootstrap CSS styling. The logo is poorly made by me. If you don't like it, make me a new one please.

The entire stack is stored in the cloud on a Bitnami MEAN (Mongo, Express, Angular, Node) stack on a "free" EC2 Linux server.

I have plans to eventually make an online .dat viewer, and what really excites me is the idea of making a 3D WebGL map which is similar to what you would find in MM Explorer but is web based.

If you have any comments, suggestions, or ideas please email the contact listed in the nav bar above. Thanks for visiting the site! Happy Mudding.
